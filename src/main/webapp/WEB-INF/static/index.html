My Photography website

personal web site to display my photos

A traveler walking on the road between Art and Photography, trying to showcase my interpretation of how I perceive each scene. For me photography is a medium to understand and to be part of Mother Nature, to learn patience while trying to be part of nature to immerse in the surrounding, being a nature photographer I mainly shoot landscapes and birds.

Born and brought up in a south Indian village in Nilgiris from child hood I was amazed to see how pretty nature organized its children, the echo system of birds, animals, lake and streams etc. Sharing is always happiness, being an amature in photography I would like to share the photos I have taken with you all. This site is an attempt to put my captures in display.

Wildlife Photography

Capturing animals in their natural habitats is known as wild life photography. The animals are often photographed in action, such as eating, fighting, or in flight,. Alternatively, more static portraits may be used to show detail of the animal or to depict it in its environment.

Macro and texture

Macro Photography is extreme close-up photography, usually of very small subjects and living organisms like insects, in which the size of the subject in the photograph is greater than life size..
